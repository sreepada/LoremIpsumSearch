Dataset

Collaborative Research: Seasonality of circumpolar tundra - ocean and atmosphere controls and effects on energy and carbon budgets
 

Weekly and Biweekly time series for tundra regions 1982-2011

  • Metadata
  • Download Data
Point of Contact:Uma Bhatt
Description:Time series for weekly surface temperature (full tundra), sea ice concentration (100-km) and ocean heat content (2.5m to 100m or the bottom) for each Treshnikov domain comprises this seasonal data set. The time series for biweekly (twice a month) Maximum Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (MaxNDVI) for the 23 Treshnikov domains are in this data set. The data cover the period from 1982-2011.

The data text files are tab delimited and column names identify the week of the year and (See main README for abbreviations and see TreshnikovJan11.png).

23 files for each of the three fields for example: beauf_MaxNDVI_biweekly_82-11_ftun.txt, beauf_seaice_weekly_82-11_100km.txt, and beauf_ts_weekly_82-11_ftun.txt

This file contains data for the Beaufort region and has 100-km series for spring sea ice concentration and full tundra average for MaxNDVI and surface temperature (ts).

Each of the 23 Treshnikov divisions (defined using Jan 2011 map) has a similar format to these files. A value of 999 signifies missing data.
